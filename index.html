Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've answered the most common ones below — or give us a call!

Do you offer free estimates? ▼

Yes! We provide estimates and are happy to guide you in choosing the right product or solution before any work begins. Just give us a call to get started.

What areas do you serve? ▼

We're based in Midland Park, NJ and serve all of Bergen County including Ridgewood, Waldwick, Wyckoff, Ho-Ho-Kus, Ramsey, Paramus, and many more. Not sure? Just call us!

What are your hours? ▼

We're open Monday through Friday, 7:30 AM – 4:30 PM. We are closed on Saturdays and Sundays. For urgent matters, give us a call and we'll do our best to help.

How quickly can you respond to an issue? ▼

We're known for fast, responsive service. Even for first-time customers, Joe has been known to arrive in record time for emergencies. We prioritize getting to you quickly when it matters.

Do you handle gas line work? ▼

Yes! We handle gas line runs, extensions, and connections including outdoor lines for grills, pool sheds, and generators. All gas work is done safely and up to code.

Will you clean up after the job? ▼

Absolutely. Our team is careful and tidy — customers consistently mention that no cleanup was needed after we finished. We respect your home as if it were our own.

Are you licensed and insured? ▼

Yes, Lomupo Plumbing & Heating LLC is a fully licensed and insured plumbing and heating contractor in New Jersey. You can hire us with complete confidence.
